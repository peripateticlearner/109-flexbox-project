A fun fact: Did you know that coffee originated in Ethiopia?! There are thousands of coffee bean varrietals. It is the only place in the world where coffee grows natively. Legend has it that a goat herder, named of Kaldi, found his goats eating cherries that had them wound up with endless energy. He showed it to some local monks who eventually figured out how to brew it!
